# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving Stassh.

Before writing code, please open an issue first to discuss the idea, scope, and approach.
Contributions that skip this discussion step may be closed without review.

By submitting a pull request, patch, or any other contribution to this repository, you agree to assign all right, title, and interest in your contribution to Lazar.

If you do not agree to these terms, please do not submit contributions.

Maintainers may refuse, close, or leave unmerged any contribution that does not satisfy this policy.
